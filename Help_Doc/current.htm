Current State Page:

This page is basically the main page of the webapp. Here, three green indications under the titles yawn , drowsiness and talk means that driver is neither yawning , nor drowsy and also not inattentive.

If driver becomes drowsy, the green bar below "drowsiness" box starts to blink a red allarm indicating "Take Rest" an side by side , voice alert is also given.
If driver yawns, the green bar below "Yawn" box starts to blink a red allarm indicating "Be Attentive" and side by side , voice alert is also given.

If driver is talking continiously, the green bar below "Talk" box starts to blink a red allarm indicating "Please be attentive" and side by side , voice alert is also given

The driver has to keep this page open all the time in the display so that he as well as passengers can easily visualize the changing states of driver and take actions accordingly to ensure safe driving.
